About us

Our Company is presently engaged in placement of debt securities like bonds, debentures to PF Trusts & Superannuation Trusts of Public Sector Undertakings and Corporates, Education Institutions, Finance companies, Individual Investors, etc. We have track record of regularly dealing with such clients and we get regular orders and queries from them for their requirements of debt securities. etc
We are SEBI registered Stock Broker with registration number INZ000313233 and BSE registered Trading Member on New Debt Segment .We are also an Online Bond Platform Providers (OBPPs) member of debt market segment at BSE.

Ravi Kant Mathur

Our Promoter and Director Mr. Ravi Kant Mathur has experience of approximately 23 years in financial services, merchant banking & debt securities market. Prior to promoting Dimension Financial, he worked with Bajaj Capital Ltd for 3 years in Merchant Banking, Marketing of public issue of equity & bonds and private placement of debt securities issued by public sector undertakings and state government undertakings. Thereafter, he worked as Asst Vice President in SPA Merchant Bankers Limited (Now SPA Capital Advisors Limited) in merchant banking division handling public issues of both debt & equity and private placement of debt securities. He has experience in preparation of offer documents as well as in marketing of issues and distribution of financial products.
