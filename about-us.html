About us

Dimension Financial Solutions Private Limited was formed by professionals in field of finance and industry with an objective to provide comprehensive range of financial services to business enterprise in India. Dimension Financial Solutions Private Limited was established by experienced professionals in the finance industry with the objective of providing a comprehensive range of financial services to businesses across India.

The company is a Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)-registered stockbroker under the BSE New Debt Segment, specializing in the debt market and offering a wide range of financial products. Dimension Financial Solutions Private Limited provides end-to-end personalized investment management services, including planning, advisory, execution, and monitoring, to help clients achieve their financial goals efficiently.

We were established in 2009 by a team of professionals who have vast experience in the field of debt advisory, debt placement and marketing of public issues of debt and equity. The current board of directors includes high experienced professionals in the field of merchant banking, debt advisory, private placement of bonds and debentures.
