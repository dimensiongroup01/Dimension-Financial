About-us

Dimension Financial Solutions Private Limited is a SEBI-registered Merchant Banker and Stock Broker (debt market) . We are committed to deliver comprehensive financial advisory and capital market services. As a trusted partner in financial Industry, we specialize in navigating complex financial landscapes to create tailored solutions that drives sustainable growth, operational efficiency, and capital optimization. Our commitment to excellence, deep regulatory understanding, and client-centric approach position us as the partner of choice for corporates, institutions, provident fund Trusts, upcoming enterprises and retail investors for there financial requirements including those related to capital market and as well in respect of investments. We believe in building enduring relationships , empowering growth, and shaping a prosperous financial future for all our stakeholders . Our TEAM consists of Highly experienced professionals in the field of Investment Banking, Debt Advisory and Marketing of financial Products. The Team Members have deep domain expertise in capital markets , financial analysis , and strategic advisory. This allows us to deliver optimal solutions that address the specific needs of each client.

We are one of the leading player of Debt Securities Market and a SEBI registered Stock Broker and a Trading Member on BSE New Debt Segment . Since 2009, we are actively engaged in placement of debt securities like bonds, debentures issued by PSUs/ State level undertaking/ Corporates and as well as government securities to provident fund /superannuation trusts, corporates, educational bodies, commercial banks and High Net Worth Investors. We also assist the Provident Fund and Superannuation Trust in assessing their portfolio and as well as facilitate them in investment/ sales of debt securities as per prevalent policy/guidelines issued by the competent authority. In addition, we have started running an Online Bonds Platform Provider (OBPPS)- BONDS ADDA for transition in debt segment.
