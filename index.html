About-us

We are the one of the leader player of Debt Security Market.Since inception, we are actively engaged in placement of debt securities like bonds, debentures issued by PSUs/ State level undertaking/ Corporates and as well as government securities to provident fund /superannuation trusts, corporates, educational bodies, commercial banks and High Net Worth Investors. We also assist the Provident Fund and Superannuation Trust in assessing their portfolio and as well as facilitate them in investment/ sales of debt securities as per prevalent policy/guidelines issued by the competent authority. The year 2017 has been a major event in our history as we moved into a new and large building located at the current address owned by us. Over more than 16 years we have been dealing with well-known and prestigious clients . On July 06 2023, we were issued registration by SEBI as Stock Broker with registration number INZ000313233 and got registered with BSE in July 2023 as Trading Member on New Debt Segment (Member Code 6824). We are also an Online Bond Platform Providers (OBPPs) member of debt market segment. We have a track record of profitability since inception.
The current Board of Directors includes highly experienced professionals in the field of merchant banking, private placement of bonds & debentures and marketing of financial products.
We source the debt securities from the market i.e. from other intermediaries, finance companies,Banks, primary dealers and PF Trusts, etc. The Government Securities are sourced from RBI directly and from primary dealers
